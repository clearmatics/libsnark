Detailed Description

Declaration of interfaces for Fp6 gadgets.

The gadgets verify field arithmetic in Fp6 = Fp3[Y]/(Y^2-X) where Fp3 = Fp[X]/(X^3-non_residue) and non_residue is in Fp.

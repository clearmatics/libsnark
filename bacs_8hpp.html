Detailed Description

Declaration of interfaces for:

  • a BACS variable assignment,
  • a BACS gate,
  • a BACS primary input,
  • a BACS auxiliary input,
  • a BACS circuit.

Above, BACS stands for "Bilinear Arithmetic Circuit Satisfiability".
